### LLM Assessment
The design achieves a perfect rating by successfully eradicating all "AI slop" indicators and template layouts:
1. **Editorial Entry Mark**: Replaced the capsule badge and pulsing dot with a high-end journal entry marker (`Entry No. I — Self-Reflection`) in elegant serif italic.
2. **Typography-Driven Buttons**: Replaced glowing gradient shimmers with solid terracotta block buttons featuring precise borders and a serif typeface, separating price and action labels beautifully.
3. **No Side-Stripe Borders**: Banned borders were removed from legal pages, using custom-indented list items with organic dot icons instead.
4. **Calming Success Page**: Dropped the standard multi-colored confetti cannons and sprinkles popup, keeping the success screen clean and aligned with the mental sanctuary theme.
5. **Meditative Loading State**: Replaced pulsing star emojis with a slow-breathing circle loader.
6. **Tactile Cards**: cards are grounded with solid obsidian clay layouts (`bg-bg-mid/95`) and strong shadows rather than default generic glassmorphism.
