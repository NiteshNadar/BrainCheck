# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Individuals looking for a thoughtful, personal, and private mental wellness self-assessment to understand their mind (stress, focus, energy, emotional balance, self-confidence) in a warm, journal-like, reflective space.

## Product Purpose
A 12-question mental wellness self-assessment tool (Braincheck) that delivers personalized wellness insights straight to their inbox for Rs. 49, with zero permanent storage of their personal information and answers.

## Brand Personality
Intimate, reflective, warm, literary, comforting, and deeply personal. "Amber & Obsidian" theme.

## Anti-references
- No cold, industrial, or medical clinical tones.
- No flashy tech startup "neon on pitch black" or generic "SaaS-cream" interfaces.
- No intrusive popups or disruptive modals.
- No forced account creation or persistent logins.

## Design Principles
1. **The Sanctuary Space**: Create an environment that feels like a quiet, warm library or a personal journal, promoting calm and honest self-reflection.
2. **Warmth & Human Touch**: Avoid stark clinical grids. Use book-like serif typography, organic earth tones, and soft lighting effects.
3. **Respect for Privacy**: Keep data collection completely transparent, minimal, and non-intrusive.
4. **Frictional flow-state**: Guide the user seamlessly through the flow (Home -> Survey -> Details -> Checkout -> Success) with smooth, natural transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- High-contrast, warm colors that are gentle on the eyes yet highly readable.
- Keyboard-accessible controls with clean focus states.
- Clear and helpful form validation and loading feedback.
