## Anti-Patterns Verdict

### LLM Assessment
- **AI Slop Verdict**: **Clean**. The success flow is updated to use slow-drifting golden embers instead of generic canvas-confetti, fitting the Obsidian & Amber brand identity perfectly.
- **Over-Transitioning & Motion fatigue**:
  1. **3D Tilt React Re-renders**: **Resolved**. The mousemove calculations in both `PricingSection` and `SuccessPage` now perform direct style property modifications on refs instead of updating React state, reducing component re-renders to zero.
  2. **Sluggish 3D Survey Page-Turn**: **Resolved**. Simplified the horizontal slide to 20px and replaced bouncy spring easing with a refined cubic-bezier curve. Swapping timeout to 220ms makes the survey feel incredibly snappy.
  3. **High-Stiffness Springs**: **Resolved**. Bouncy text hover springs have been swapped for refined ease-out-quart curves.

- **CLI Scan**: Passed.
- **Visual Contrast**: Passed.

## Overall Impression
The visual style is highly premium, intimate, and comforting. The "Amber & Obsidian" palette feels like a real journal. The interactions and page loads are smooth, hardware-accelerated, and respect user accessibility settings.

## What's Working
- **Serif Typography**: The typography hierarchy is highly refined.
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Clean and responsive keyboard mappings in the survey.
- **Hardware-Accelerated 3D Tilts**: Fluid 3D tilt cards with zero CPU layout thrashing.
- **Thematic Embers**: Drift animation on success matches the sanctuary atmosphere.
